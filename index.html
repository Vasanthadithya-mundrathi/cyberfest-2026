EVENTS & ACTIVITIES

Panel Talks

Engaging discussions with cybersecurity experts, industry leaders, and policymakers on the latest trends and challenges in digital security.

Workshops

Hand-on training sessions covering ethical hacking, penetration testing, cryptography, and other essential cybersecurity skills.

Hackathon

24-hour coding marathon to develop innovative solutions for real-world cybersecurity challenges. Prizes for top teams.

Capture The Flag

Competitive cybersecurity challenge where participants solve security-related puzzles and exploit vulnerabilities to capture flags.

Startup Expo

Showcase of innovative cybersecurity startups with live demos, networking opportunities, and investor meetings.
